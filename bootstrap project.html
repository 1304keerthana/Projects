Smart Automation Shoe

Helping the Blind Walk Safely with Confidence

About the Project

The Smart Automation Shoe is designed for visually impaired individuals. Using ultrasonic sensors and vibration feedback, it helps detect obstacles and alerts the user in real time.

Detailed Information

Purpose: Enhance independence, mobility, and safety of visually impaired individuals by integrating advanced smart technology into footwear.

Changes in User Lives: Increased Mobility, Enhanced Safety, Improved Social Interaction, Access to Opportunities, Reduced Dependence on Caregivers.

Changes in Society: Economic Benefits, Technological Awareness, Reduced Caregiver Burden, Policy and Infrastructure Improvements.

Economic Prosperity: Employment opportunities, independence in mobility, market expansion, savings.

Environmental Protection: Use of sustainable materials, reduced vehicle usage, repairable design.

  • Ultrasonic Sensors – Detect obstacles and measure distances
  • Vibration Motors – Provide tactile feedback
  • GPS & Mapping – Turn-by-turn navigation
  • Voice Assistant Integration – Voice commands and feedback
  • Cloud Connectivity – Data storage and analytics
  • Programming: Java, jQuery (frontend), Backend development

  • Obstacle Detection & Navigation
  • Location & GPS Assistance
  • Emergency SOS Assistance
  • Environmental Awareness (weather, road conditions)
  • Customizable Feedback Modes
  • Accessibility Features with Voice Assist
  • Pairing & Connection Management
  • Real-Time Navigation & Alerts

Estimated Development Cost: ₹12,00,000 – ₹24,00,000

  • Shoe Prototype Development: ₹1–2 Lakh
  • Material Selection & Testing: ₹1–2 Lakh
  • Electronics Integration: ₹2–5 Lakh
  • Prototyping (3D printing/local fabrication): ₹3–5 Lakh
  • App Development (basic features): ₹5–10 Lakh

Cost-Cutting Strategies: Use off-the-shelf components, develop in phases (MVP first), partner with startups, leverage open-source tools, and source materials locally.
